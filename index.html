How to Play:

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move
  • Dodge enemies coming from all sides
  • Survive 30-second rounds
  • Enemies spawn in rows, columns, and staggered patterns
  • Each completed round gives bonus points

Tip: Stay near the center for better reaction time!

Controls

W / ↑ Move Up
A / ← Move Left
S / ↓ Move Down
D / → Move Right
P Pause Game
Click Move to Position

Round Patterns

  • 10 Rounds Total - Survive them all!
  • Round 1: Basic enemies only
  • Round 3: Chasers appear (follow → explode)
  • Round 5: Splitters and waves
  • Round 7: Stalkers appear (follow → pause → explode)
  • Round 10: Final boss round!
  • Enemy speed increases each round
