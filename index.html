How to Play:

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move
  • Dodge enemies coming from all sides
  • Survive 30-second rounds
  • Enemies spawn in rows, columns, and staggered patterns
  • Each completed round gives bonus points

Tip: Stay near the center for better reaction time!

Controls

W / ↑ Move Up
A / ← Move Left
S / ↓ Move Down
D / → Move Right
P Pause Game
Click Move to Position

Round Patterns

  • Single: Random enemy spawns
  • Rows: 3-7 enemies in horizontal lines
  • Columns: 3-7 enemies in vertical lines
  • Staggered: Delayed enemy waves
  • Waves: Multiple patterns at once
  • Chasers: Follow you then explode!
  • Patterns intensify as round progresses
